All about me

My name is Ilan Cheraki, I am 16 years old and I live in Bologna, Italy. I was born in the Parisien suburbs in France, and five years ago I moved to California, in the United States of America. This year, my family and I moved to Bologna, Italy because of my dad’s job. I speak English (as a second language), French (as a mother tongue), and I am currently trying to learn Spanish and Italian. My passions are: playing water polo, figure painting, reading (non-fiction history and science-fiction) listening to music (heavy metal, rock and roll), playing drums (mostly jazz and rock), driving motorcycles, and hiking. Academically, I cannot stop studying history, and lately I have been particularly interested in Economics.
